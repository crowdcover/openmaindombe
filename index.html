Open Mai Ndombe

An open platform to increase transparency in the Mai Ndombe region.

OpenMaindombe aims to have the most up to date mapping information regarding land use planning in the Mai Ndombe region in the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Through data sharing agreements with government agencies, international NGOs such as WRI and WWF, and REDD+ project implementers, we have assembled a comprehensive public database of information for a critical region for REDD+ and FLEG/FLEGT as well extractive industries, infrastructure, and agriculture. Community territories have also been provided by REDD+ project proponents as well as local NGOs. If you would like to learn how you can contribute new maps or edit existing ones, go here.

Open Mai Ndombe - About

OpenMaindombe is a collaborative initiative that aims to strengthen land use transparency in Mai Ndombe region of the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC).

Aim

OpenMaindombe aims to increase transparency in land use planning in OpenMaindombe. Transparency is critical for effective decision making but currently, there is no single platform bringing data from public, private, and charitable institutions.

Goals

1. Strengthen collaboration between the FLEG/FLEGT and REDD+ sectors in MaiNdombe

2. Create “one map” of community mapping territories

Why Mai Ndombe

With three pilot REDD+ projects and 20 logging concessions, Mai Ndombe is at the center both the REDD+ and industrial logging industry in DRC. How it Works

OpenMaindombe is an open data community hosting a wide variety of maps, reports, and documents related to land use planning in the Mai Ndombe area. All of the mapping information, stories, and resources are free to download, use and publish provided you follow the attribution terms.
